Stone Crusher Unit Plant

As we all know, the mining industry and stone crushing industry play very important role in the national economics. It is of great importance for mine owners and quarry concentrators to choose a suitable crushing unit plant.

crushers in a crushing plant

In the stone crusher unit plant, a jaw crusher is used as primary crusher and impact crusher is used as secondary crusher. A vibrating feeder is provided to send raw materials into jaw crusher, and a vibrating screen is added after impact crusher for product selection. We also supply a complete conveyor system channel and angle type construction for materials transport.

HP series cone crusher with hydraulic system can simplify the operation and promote efficiency. HP cone crusher manufactured by Zenith Company is widely used to process hard minerals like iron ore, granite rock, hard limestone, gold, manganese, concrete etc.

crushing process of a crusher unit plant

There are several types of crushers to be choose as stone aggregate crusher machine used in stone aggregate crushing plant. The Big materials are fed to the jaw crusher evenly and gradually by vibrating feeder through a hopper for the primary crushing. After first crushing, the material will transferred to impact crusher or cone crusher by belt conveyor for secondary crushing; the crushed materials will then transferred to vibrating screen for separating. After being separated, the parts that can meet standard will be taken away as final products, while the other parts will be returned to cone crusher or impact crusher for re-crushing , thus forming a closed circuit. Size of final products can be combined and graded according to customer’s specific requirement. We can also equip dust catcher system to protect environment.
