stone crushing process flow

Big dimension stones are transferred to primary crusher through Vibrating Feeder from hopper for 1st crushing, then the crushed resources are transferred to Impact Crusher by Belt Conveyor for secondary crushing. The crushed materials is going to be transferred for the vibrating screen, and separated to distinct sizes. Those aggregate with suitable size is going to be transferred to the last merchandise pile and individuals with unsuitable size is going to be transferred towards the Impact Crusher for re-crushing. This types a closed circuit manifold cycles. The sizes of final merchandise is going to be graded and separated in accordance with customers' demands, and also the deduster will likely be attached for that sake of setting protection.

The process concerned is usually to feed the stone in for the Hammer Crushers to generate it more more compact in dimension as needed with the consumer. Within the hammer crusher, the stone is crushed. The crushed stone is screened to separate the produce in unique sizes from the separator. The crushed stone is conveyed with the conveyors to trucks for transport towards the market place spot or storage place.
