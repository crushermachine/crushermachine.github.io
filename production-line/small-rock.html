Small Rock Crusher Supplier

introduction

As we all know, stone crusher is a crushing equipment designed to breaking large scale rocks into smaller sizes. when speaking of small rock crusher, we usually mean the following equipment: jaw crusher, impact crusher, hammer crusher and cone crusher. The small mobile rock crusher(mobile crushing and screening plants) is composed of primary crushing and screening station and secondary one, belt conveyor, etc. Every crushing station is an independent working unit, they can respectively assume their different responsibility, and the belt conveyor is responsible for material transmitting and piling up between each crushing station.

applications

Area: widely used in mine, metallurgy, construction, road building, railway, water conservancy and chemical industry.

Materials:quartz,rock, gold, basalt, limestone, dolomite, calcite, pyrite, chalk , anthracite, fluorite and other materials whose compressive strength is lower than 350mpa.

small rock crusher types
