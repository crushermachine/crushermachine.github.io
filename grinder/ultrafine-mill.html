XZM ultrafine mill is known as an extremely thin powder grinding mill designed according to our numerous years of experience and sophisticated grinding technologies. We take in positive aspects from the same trade abroad and at home. We evaluate analysis and attempt on 12 types of mills and horizontal hammer crushers. And ultimately we produced this high-efficiency and low-cost super thin mill by investigation and trial.

applications

The machine can produce thin powder and super thin powder. It is applied in non-explosive and non-flammable materials with hardness less than 6, such as, calcite, limestone, chalk, kaolin, dolomite, bentonite, talcum, talc, magnesite, illite, barite, gypsum, alum earth, graphite, and fluorite. The final end products’ size can be adjusted between 325~2500 mesh.

features and advantages

High efficiency of this super thin grinding mill. Under the same condition of finished particle size and power of motor, the output of super thin grinding mill is 40% higher than airflow mill and whisk mill.

High safety. No axle tree or bolt is used in the super thin grinding chamber, so there will be no worn out problem of bearing and sealing parts, and also no loose of bolts.

The finished particles produced by this super thin grinding mill are super thin and have a good uniformity of fineness.

High using rate of wear parts

Environmental protection. This super thin grinding mill is environmental friendly. Pollution and noise are lightened by using deduster and muffler so as to reach the requirement of national environmental protection.
