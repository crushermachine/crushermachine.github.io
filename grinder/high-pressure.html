high pressure grinding mill introduction

A grinding mill is a unit operation designed to break solid materials into smaller pieces. There are many types of grinding mill, such as ball mill, Raymond mill, MTM trapezium grinding machine, high pressure suspension grinding mill and so on. As an ideal grinding mill for secondary crushing, high pressure suspension grinding mill is an efficient pulverizer machine for fine powder grinding. This high pressure suspension grinding mill can grind many kinds of mine and other materials, such as the cement, the silicate product, new type building material, fire-proof material, chemical fertilizer, black and non-ferrous metal, glass, ceramics and etc.

Features And Advantages

High productivity, high efficiency. Compared with other pulverizers under the same power conditions,the output of this machine is increased by 20-30%.

The final product of this high pressure suspension grinding mill is super thin.

A wide range of application. All the mineral materials with Mohs hardness below 9.3 can be crushed.

Environmental-friendly. Its dust-removing effect exactly meets the National Dust Discharge Standard.

simple operating and easy maintenance
