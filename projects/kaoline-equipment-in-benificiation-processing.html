kaolin processing and crushing method

In earlier mechanized opencast mines, processing involved was crushing to required size and separation of various items by dry screening. With all the rising emphasis on cleaner item, wet screening has come in vogue in location of dry screening. Small capacity crushers have now offered place to big capacity crushers with enhanced reduction ratio. From Jaw crushers, mine operations have switched over to gyratory and cone crushers exactly where closely sized materials are necessary. Given that steel plants are switching more than to sinter, kaolin fines are now obtaining marketplace and full recovery of these fines, classifiers, hydro cyclones and filters are increasingly employed. For obtaining kaolin concentrates from low grade ore many processing routes of gravity separation, flotation and magnetic separations are in practice by way of out the globe and India as well.
