A basic ultra fine mill

In the mining industry, the need for efficient fine grinding processes is increasing rapidly. As demand for ores with finer mineral intergrowth rises, grinding technology is being developed to keep up.

stirred milling

Stirred milling development work in the mining industry up until this time has been limited to that related to simply adopting the technology and making it work in high throughput, low value product conditions: larger mill sizes, low cost grinding media, wear protection, etc. Only limited work on the fundamentals has been undertaken and there remains many important issues of stirred milling operation which are not well understood by the industry.

features and benefits
