double roller type magnetic separator

Finer material is in almost all applications preferred to be processed in water. Also for the wet processing there are a number of models to choose from to suit the various requirements. For all wet processing separators the wet low intensity magnetic separator is selected to achieve best performance in each individual case.

Double roller type magnetic separator is used in the mining, coal preparation factory, etc. It is used to separate the ultrafine particles of strongly-magnetic materials from the low- intensity magnetic field or to separate the strongly-magnetic materials from the non-magnetic materials.

Advantages and Features

  • Powerful magnetic intensity
  • Dust and rain protection
  • Robust industrial structure
  • Manufactured with customized specification
  • Easy installation and maintenance
  • Wear Protection For double roller Magnetic Separator

operations

The slurry fed into the separators is very abrasive, so all tanks, boxes and launders are lined inside with rubber. The revolving drum is lined with either rubber or stainless steel and its ends are fitted with specially designed end collars. At some applications these wear protection measures need to be reinforced by increase of liner thickness or replacement by even tougher material such as ceramics or polyurethane. With wear protection system, the magnetic separator works stable and durable.
