Sand washing plant

Sand washing machine is washing equipment for artificial sand (including all-natural sand). It could wash stone powder and residual away from the mixed sands created by sand generating machine, which can improve the sand good quality largely. Thus turn into vital equipment in sand producing production line. It is actually mainly used in sand producing factory, mining, construction, chemistry, water conservancy and hydroelectric power projects and concrete batching plant.

Aggregate washing plant is a kind of cleaning equipment of international advanced level for sand and slag pellets, it is developed on the basis of introducing foreign outstanding technology of the same kind of products.

Sand washing plant applications

It is widely used for cleaning materials in the following industries: quarry, minerals, building materials, transportation, chemical industry, water conservation and hydropower, cement mixture station and so on.
