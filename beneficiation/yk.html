Vibrating screen introduction

The vibrating equipment is an efficient new, multi-layer vibration equipment for stone quarries and sand filters. Vibrating screen designed by our company is a kind of screening equipment, in other word, vibrating screener is a machine tool to separate aggregates by particle size by means of an exciting force applied to the machine causing it to vibrate. Zenith's vibrating screen can be divided into the following types: inclined screen, horizontal screen, YK series vibrating screen, triple-shafted horizontal screen and high speed screen.

Mobile vibrating screen features

1, the use of tilt angle structure, small amplitude, high frequency, high efficiency of the device screen.

2, excitation power, the exciting force by block eccentric.

3, the use of high strength bolts with a sieve screen box beams will be combined effective and smooth running.

4,Simple and dependable Eccentric type system; High screening capacity;High durability;No transmission of screen panels;Ready changing of screen plates;Rigid and vibrating resistance screen body.

Applications

Mobile vibrating screen is widely applied to screen dry materials in ore dressing, building materials, concrete, rock, crushed stone, quarry waste, ore mineral mining, construction, demolition debris, aggregate, dewatering industrial, sand and gravel etc. The advantaged of this series screen are as follows: stable operation, lower energy consumption, low noise, high efficiency and long service life.
