what is flotation separation?

Flotation is a separation method based on the wettability of minerals, which separates the minerals by their difference of floatability. In modern flotation, the potions play a very important role. After the particular treatment of potions, the mineral floatability has been changed. So we can make the selected minerals float on the mineralized forth, paving the way for later separation.
